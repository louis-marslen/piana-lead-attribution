# French regions → department codes

Used to expand the geographic scope of a NAF × geo bucket when wins concentrate in only one or two departments of a larger region.

When a libellé NAF has wins in department X, consider including:
- The full region X belongs to (for moderate confidence)
- Adjacent regions if national reach is needed (low confidence — only as last resort)
